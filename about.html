    I am Coda Bool, I am a student at University of Central Florida. I have always had a passion for photography. I shoot all events and would love to help you get that perfect album for your next occasion.


    I have been collecting camera gear and as many tools as I can get. These tools help me with every project. Many parts like camera sliders and light reflectors help to make video or make a great shot even better. Combining good photography with powerful software like Photoshop allow me to create lasting images.


    Photographs can tell an entire story. Changing your mood and provoking memories which can be very powerful. I would love to become a part of that experience for you. An important part of photography is the collaboration. When working with people I like to become your friend and help find exactly what it is you would like to see. This is about the story you want to tell and I am happy to be a part of that. The details of your story are important and I want to make sure everything is communicated and can be delivered back to you in my images. When approaching a project in this perspective I find it works to deliver something which everyone can enjoy for a lifetime. This is why I find my work so important and cannot wait to begin collaboration with you!


    My porfolio work is just beginning but I already have images which I am very proud of. I have been taking photos for family events for years. I have three brothers who are all married and have children. My family trusts me to capture their events, be it baby photos, wedding photos, or group family photography. I would love to share that same passion I have for capturing my family and applying it to events within your family. Together we can create a photo album which can be used forever and will honor your memory of any occasion. Your event is important and I cannot wait to become a part of it!
